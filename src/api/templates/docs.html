Parameters

All parameters are optional.

series

The requested series rates.
Strings separated by commas
Possible series for Bank of Canada:
'prime_rate', 'one_year_conventional_mortgage', 'three_year_conventional_mortgage', 'five_year_conventional_mortgage'
Possible series for Federal Reserve Economic Data:
'prime_rate', 'five_year_average_mortgage', 'fifteen_year_average_mortgage', 'thirty_year_average_mortgage'

start_date

The earliest date for series data.
YYYY-MM-DD formatted string.

end_date

The latest date for series data.
YYYY-MM-DD formatted string.

hide_none

Whether to hide entries with any null values
'true' or 'false'

offset

how many entries to skip.
integer.

limit

The maximum amount of entries to show.
integer.
